Find out which countries have won the most medals and how the participation of nations has changed over time. From 776 BC to 393 AD, the ancient Olympic Games were held in Olympia, Greece. After 1503 years, it reappeared. In 1896, the first modern Olympics were held in Athens, Greece. All of the Games from Athens 1896 to Rio 2016 are referred to as the “Modern Olympics.” Between 1912 and 1920, and 1936 and 1948, there are two long periods without any Games, corresponding to WWI and WWII.
